Aeon for Ocean

Teaching Kids to Lead the Way in Ocean Conservation

Project Overview


Objective

The Bootcamp App is a one stop shop for students to gain access to class materials and stay abreast of upcoming assignments

Problem Statement

Reasearch shows that students attending Bootcamp courses often struggle with accessing and navigating materials for the course. Because of the fast-paced nature of the course, coupled with their busy schedules, it is imperative for students to find was to organiz their work. How might we design a space that will allow students to easily access course materials and stay on top of their work.
